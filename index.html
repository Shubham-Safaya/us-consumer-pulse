Sources & method. Bureau of Labor Statistics Public Data API (CPI-U series CUUR0000SA0/SAF1/SETB01/SAH1; LNS14000000; LNS11300000; CES0500000003; CES4200000001) and US Census Bureau ACS 1-year estimates. Pulled daily at 11:00 UTC by an open-source GitHub Action; derived metrics (YoY, real wages) computed in the open.
All data are US federal government works in the public domain (17 U.S.C. §105) accessed through official public APIs within their terms of use. Aggregate statistics only — no personal data is collected, stored, or processed, so no CCPA/GDPR exposure. This page sets no cookies and runs no trackers.
